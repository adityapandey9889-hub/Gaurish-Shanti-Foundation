Who We Are

Gaurish Shanti Foundation is a registered social welfare organization (Society) established with the vision of serving humanity and contributing to the holistic development of society. The foundation works with a strong commitment to uplift underprivileged, marginalized, and vulnerable sections of the community by promoting equality, social justice, peace, and self-reliance
